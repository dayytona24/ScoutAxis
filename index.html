Deep Metrics

EVERY NUMBER EXPLAINED

Scout Axis doesn't just surface numbers — every metric has a defined formula and a scouting purpose.

Power Score (0–1000)
40% Placement + 25% Consistency + 15% Peak + 10% Finals + 5% Volume. Recency-weighted — last 5 events matter more than the first.
Scout Score & Prospect Index
Composite signal: momentum × ceiling × consistency × event trust. Penalised for regression. Surfaces players before their rank reflects their real trajectory.
Volatility & Regression
Std dev of percentile scores (boom/bust index) + early vs. late season trend split. Four severity levels: None, Mild, Moderate, Severe.
Advanced Combat Stats
Avg elims, damage, EPPG, PPPG, and EPS (elim performance score) — loaded per event where Osirion data is available.
